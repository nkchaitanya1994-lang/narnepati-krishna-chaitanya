Areas of Expertise

Research Focus

Engineering the intersection of microbiology, electrochemistry, and sustainability — converting CO₂ into high-value biochemicals through novel bioelectrochemical systems.

01 / CORE ⚡

Microbial Electrosynthesis (MES)

Developing bioelectrochemical cells that harness electrolithoautotrophic microorganisms to fix CO₂ into volatile fatty acids using renewable electricity as the energy driver.

02 / CORE 🌿

CO₂ Reduction & Conversion

Investigating electrochemical and biological pathways for transforming CO₂ into acetate, acetic acid, caproic acid, butyric acid, and medium chain fatty acids.

03 / APPLIED 🧪

Acetate & Acetic Acid Production

Optimizing MES reactor design, electrode materials, and microbial community enrichment to maximize acetate yields from CO₂ reduction at the cathode interface.

04 / APPLIED 🔗

Medium Chain Fatty Acids (MCFA)

Producing MCFA (caproic acid C6, caprylic acid C8) from CO₂ via chain elongation in integrated dark fermentation–MES reactors with Clostridium kluyveri.

05 / SYSTEMS 💧

Wastewater Treatment & Recovery

Integrating bioelectrochemical systems with wastewater treatment for emerging contaminant removal and circular resource recovery from waste streams.

06 / METHODS 📊

Process Optimization (RSM)

Applying Response Surface Methodology and central composite design to optimize MES parameters including pH, applied voltage, headspace pressure, and electrolyte composition.
